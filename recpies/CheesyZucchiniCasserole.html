Cheesy Zucchini Casserole

Cheesy Zucchini Casserole is a deliciously comforting dish that's perfect for using up fresh summer zucchini. Sliced zucchini is layered with rich, melty cheese—like cheddar or mozzarella—and baked with a creamy mixture of eggs and seasonings. A crispy topping of breadcrumbs or crushed crackers adds the perfect crunch. This casserole is easy to prepare and makes a great vegetarian main or hearty side dish. It's flavorful, cheesy, and satisfying enough to please the whole family. Serve it with a light salad or grilled protein for a balanced meal. It's a great way to turn simple vegetables into something special.

Ingredients
  • Vegetables
    • Zucchini (sliced, diced, or rounds)
    • Onion (chopped)
    • Garlic (minced or garlic powder)
    • Tomatoes (optional, sliced)
    • Bell pepper (optional, diced)
    • Fresh parsley or other herbs (optional)
  • Cheeses
    • Cheddar cheese
    • Parmesan cheese
    • Mozzzerella cheese
