S'mores

S'mores are a classic campfire treat made by sandwiching a roasted marshmallow and a piece of chocolate between two graham crackers. The heat melts the chocolate and softens the marshmallow, creating a gooey, sweet delight. Popular at bonfires and camping trips, s’mores evoke nostalgia and fun. Simple to make yet irresistibly tasty, they’re a beloved dessert that captures the essence of cozy, outdoor gatherings with friends and family.

Ingredients
  • Graham crackers
  • Marshmallows
  • chocolate
