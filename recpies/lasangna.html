lasagna

Lasagna is a classic Italian dish made by layering sheets of pasta with rich meat or vegetable sauce, creamy béchamel or ricotta, and plenty of melted cheese. Baked until bubbly and golden, it’s a hearty, comforting meal perfect for family dinners or gatherings. Variations include vegetarian, meat lovers, and even gluten-free options. Each bite offers a delicious combination of textures and flavors that make lasagna a timeless favorite
